Meet the Real Agents of Scottsdale

Have you ever wondered what it's like to work in the real estate industry? The Real Agents of Scottsdale is a new real estate reality series that will give you a behind-the-scenes look at the successes, struggles, and triumphs of "real" real estate agents and their clients. Tune in to discover the truth behind the Scottsdale real estate market with this exciting series!

Unfiltered Reality

We're not afraid to show the truth. From showing failed deals to success stories, we're here to give you a real look at the Scottsdale area real estate market.

Experience it all

We know that no two properties are alike. Follow us as we take you on an adventure through exclusive locations giving viewers the most immersive experience possible.

Bringing You Closer

Our episodes are designed to bring you close to the action and give you a unique perspective of what it takes to be an agent in Scottsdale. We'll show you how deals work from start to finish, without any sugar coating!
